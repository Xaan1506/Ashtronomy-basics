What Ashtronomy actually is ?

Astronomy is the science that studies everything beyond Earth’s atmosphere — the Sun, Moon, stars, planets, and galaxies. It helps us understand how the universe works, how celestial bodies move, and how space and time are connected. Astronomy combines science, mathematics, and observation, and it has been studied in India for thousands of years as Jyotisha Shastra — the “Science of Light.”
